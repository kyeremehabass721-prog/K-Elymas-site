About the Author

Kyeremeh Abass is a Ghanaian inspirational and Christian author whose writings focus on faith, purpose, and personal growth. Through his books, he inspires readers to discover their God-given purpose and pursue excellence in life.

Abass developed a passion for writing at an early age. His works encourage young people and readers worldwide to build strong faith, develop discipline, and live meaningful lives guided by purpose.
